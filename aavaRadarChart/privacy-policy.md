# Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how the Power BI Custom Visual (“the Visual”) handles data. The Visual is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect, store, transmit, or process personal data outside of the user’s Power BI environment.

## 2. No Data Collection
The Visual does not:

- Collect or store any personal information.

- Transmit data to external servers, services, or third parties.

- Use cookies, analytics, tracking technologies, or telemetry.

- Access or retain user identifiers, usage logs, or device information.

All data displayed or processed by the Visual remains entirely within the user’s Power BI environment.

## 3. Data Processing Within Power BI
Any data rendered by the Visual is provided directly by Power BI. The Visual only reads the dataset passed to it by Power BI for the purpose of visualization. It does not modify, export, or persist this data.

## 4. Open Source Transparency
The Visual’s source code is publicly available under the MIT License. Users may inspect the code to verify how data is handled.

## 5. Third‑Party Services
The Visual does not connect to or interact with any third‑party services. If you choose to modify the Visual or integrate it with external components, you are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable privacy requirements.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
The Visual does not target or collect information from children. As no data is collected, no special handling is required.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Visual after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

## 8. Contact
The Visual is provided without support obligations, and the authors do not request or require user contact. If you choose to reach out voluntarily (e.g., via the public repository), any information you provide is at your discretion and subject to the platform’s own privacy terms.
